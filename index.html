How It Works

{app_name} is designed to be simple. First, you register your unlock point. This can be an RFID tag or QR code you already have, or one of the cheap/free options we have listed below. Simply scan in the app to register as your unlock point. Select from the list all the apps you would like to block, and you're done!

Every time you want to block your apps, it is just a button press away. To unblock, press unlock and scan at your unlock point.
